Things to do

Here is a non-exhaustive list of suggested things to do in Copenhagen and Helsingør

  1. Copenhagen
  2. Helsignør

Copenhagen

  • Canal tour
    • Also the best way to see the little mermaid statue - she is very little
  • Rundetårn
    • The round tower
  • Botanisk have
    • Botanical gardens by the University of Copenhagen
  • Changing of the guards at Amalienborg
  • Highly recommend the vegetarian breakfast at Paludan cafe
  • Tivoli
  • Torvehallerne food market
  • Freetown Christiania ‘free state’
  • Nyhavn
    • Expensive drinks/food, but very pretty and worth seeing
    • It’s also where the canal tours depart from
  • Main shopping street strøget
  • Bastard cafe
    • Boardgame cafe/bar
  • Mikkeller brewery
  • Reffen
    • Far to get to, but worth it for the food market and vibes

Helsingør

  • Kronborg castle
    • See the castle, casemates, art
    • Be sure to check out brew with a view coffee roastery and cafe: https://www.strandvejsristeriet.dk/
  • Værftets Madmarked food market, inside an old shipworks
  • M/S Maritime museum
  • Self-guided walking tour of Helsingør
  • Browse shops and old streets
    • There are good second hand shops in the town centre
    • Be sure to go down the old streets too!
  • Take the ferry to Sweden (Helsingborg) with ForSea
